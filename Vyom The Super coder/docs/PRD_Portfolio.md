Akash Rana — Portfolio Website

Version 1.0 | April 2026 | Prepared for: Akash Rana
1. OVERVIEW & PURPOSE
What This Site Is
A personal portfolio and thought-leadership website that serves as the central hub for Akash Rana's professional identity. It combines a showcase of automation & marketing work, a services/consulting pitch, course/training offerings, and a content blog — all under a single AI-first personal brand.
Primary Goals
Convert inbound visitors (from LinkedIn, referrals, Google search) into leads for Vyom Webwave or NIHT
Establish Akash as a credible AI Automation Architect and educator in the Indian market
Serve as a live, linkable proof-of-work portfolio for client pitches and speaking engagements
Support SEO for keywords: AI automation consultant Kolkata, digital marketing trainer India, n8n workflow expert
Act as a single link to share: LinkedIn bio, email signature, business card, course landing page
2. SITE ARCHITECTURE (PAGES & SECTIONS)
2.1 Home / Hero Section
Full-screen hero: Name, title, 1-line value prop, CTA buttons ("See My Work" + "Let's Talk")
Animated background: subtle particle/network animation or abstract AI visual — dark navy theme
Scrolling marquee of logos: tools/platforms used (n8n, Claude, Google Ads, Meta, Python, etc.)
Social proof row: X years experience | X professionals trained | X clients served | X automation workflows built
2.2 About Me
Professional photo (headshot, clean background)
3-paragraph bio: origin story → current work → vision/mission
Timeline: key career milestones (NIHT founding, Vyom Webwave, first AI project, etc.)
Dual identity section: 'As an Educator' vs 'As a Consultant' — two columns
Personal touch: Tanjore art interest, Kolkata roots, community impact in training
2.3 Services (Vyom Webwave Offerings)
3 service cards: AI Automation & Workflows | Performance Marketing | Digital Strategy & SEO
Each card: icon, 2-line description, "What you get" bullet list (3–5 items), CTA
Featured service highlight: n8n Agentic Workflow Design — full case-study style breakdown
Pricing strategy: 'Custom Pricing — Book a Discovery Call' (no fixed rates on page)
Trust signals: client logos, testimonial pull-quote, turnaround time stats
2.4 Portfolio / Case Studies
Card grid (6 projects minimum) with category filter: Automation | Marketing | EdTech | Dev
Project cards: thumbnail, title, short description, tech stack tags, 'View Case Study' link
Case Study pages (3 deep-dives minimum): Problem → Approach → Solution → Outcome
Must include: B2B Lead Scraping System, n8n Google Ads Pipeline, NIHT SEO Audit, AI Training Course
GitHub link on relevant projects; live demo links where available
2.5 Training & Courses (NIHT / Akash's Courses)
Course showcase: 6-Week AI Agents Course, 4-Week AI for Entrepreneurs, Digital Marketing Certification
Each course card: duration, level, curriculum highlights, enrollment CTA
"Train Your Team" B2B section — targeting Corporate HR/L&D (reuse NIHT B2B sales deck content)
Student testimonials carousel
Upcoming batch dates + WhatsApp/email enrollment form
2.6 Blog / Insights
Articles on: n8n workflows, AI tools for marketers, automation case studies, prompt engineering
Minimum 3 pillar posts at launch; monthly cadence post-launch
SEO-optimised: target long-tail keywords in AI automation + digital marketing India space
Categories: AI Automation | Marketing | Education | Tools & Reviews
Author card on each post with subscribe CTA
2.7 Contact / Let's Work Together
Short form: Name, Email, Company, Inquiry type (Consulting / Training / Speaking / Other), Message
Direct links: WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Email
Location: Kolkata, India (with note: available remote globally)
Response time promise: "I reply within 24 hours"
Optional: Calendly embed for discovery call scheduling

Build and deploy: Hero, About, Contact pages

Phase 2 — Core Content (Week 3–4)
Build Services page with 3 service cards
Build Portfolio page with 6 project cards (2 with full case studies)
Add Courses/Training section
Integrate contact form with email notification (Resend)
Write 2 SEO blog posts and publish
Phase 3 — Polish & Promote (Week 5–6)
Add animations: scroll-triggered reveals, hover states, hero particle bg
Complete 2 more case studies (NIHT SEO audit, AI Training Course)
Performance audit: Core Web Vitals pass check
Update LinkedIn with website URL, add to email signature
Share launch post on LinkedIn; target 500+ impressions on launch week
Phase 4 — Scale (Month 2–3)
Launch blog with monthly cadence (2 posts/month)
Add testimonials section with real client/student quotes
Explore Calendly integration for discovery call booking
Set up Google Ads retargeting pixel for future campaigns
Review analytics, adjust content based on top-performing pages
